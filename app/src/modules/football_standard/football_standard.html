General idea

The general idea is that there's a collection of clubs,
out of which a lineup for a specific match is constructed.

Each club (obviously) has:
  • A logo (a bitmap image)
  • Official Title
  • Official Title abbreviation (Title shortened to 3 letters)
  • Players
  • And the main coach
